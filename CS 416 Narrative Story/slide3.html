State Housing Market Recovery and Appreciation

As we examine the post-crisis recovery, this slide provides a detailed look at how different states fared. The scatter plot shows the 5-year appreciation, peak-to-current, and peak-to-trough changes in home prices for each state.

Hover over the points to see specific data for each state. The scatter plot reveals that states like Nevada and Washington saw significant appreciation, indicating strong recovery and growth, while states like Connecticut and New Jersey have not fully recovered.
